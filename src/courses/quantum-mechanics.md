## Course Description

An introduction to quantum mechanics covering wave functions, the Schrödinger equation, and applications to simple systems.

## Prerequisites

- Classical Mechanics
- Differential Equations
- Linear Algebra

## Topics Covered

1. Wave-Particle Duality
2. Schrödinger Equation
3. Quantum Operators
4. Hydrogen Atom
5. Angular Momentum
6. Perturbation Theory

## Course Materials

- Textbook: Introduction to Quantum Mechanics by Author Name
- Problem Sets: Weekly assignments
