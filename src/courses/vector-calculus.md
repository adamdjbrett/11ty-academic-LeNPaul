## Course Description

An introduction to vector calculus covering topics such as vector fields, line integrals, surface integrals, and the theorems of Green, Stokes, and Gauss.

## Prerequisites

- Calculus I and II
- Linear Algebra

## Topics Covered

1. Vector Fields
2. Line Integrals
3. Surface Integrals
4. Green's Theorem
5. Stokes' Theorem
6. Divergence Theorem
