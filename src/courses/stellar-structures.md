## Course Description

An advanced course on the physics of stellar interiors and evolution, covering topics such as stellar formation, nuclear reactions, and stellar death.

## Prerequisites

- Thermodynamics
- Quantum Mechanics
- Astrophysics I

## Topics Covered

1. Stellar Formation
2. Hydrostatic Equilibrium
3. Nuclear Reactions in Stars
4. Stellar Evolution
5. White Dwarfs and Neutron Stars
6. Supernovae

- Textbook: Stellar Structure and Evolution by Author Name
- Research Papers: Selected readings
